Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

What is SCHOMART?

SCHOMART is a student-focused e-commerce platform that allows students and others to buy and sell products and services within university campuses and local communities in Nigeria. It's a convenient, safe, and reliable marketplace for everything from textbooks and electronics to freelance services and housing.

How do I create an account?

To create an account, visit the "Sign Up" page . You can register using your email address. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete your profile.

Is SCHOMART free to use?

Yes, creating an account, browsing listings, and posting a certain number of basic ads are completely free. We also offer premium services and subscription plans for users who want to increase their visibility and get more features.

How do I post an advert?

After logging in, go to the "Sell" or "Post Advert" section. Fill in the details of your item, including title, description, category, price, and photos. You can choose a free or paid plan to publish your ad.

How long does it take for my advert to be approved?

Most ads are reviewed and approved within 24 hours. Our moderation team checks each ad to ensure it complies with our posting policies and safety guidelines.

What happens if my advert is rejected?

If your advert is rejected, we will send you a notification with the reason for rejection. You can edit your ad to address the issues and resubmit it for review.

Can I edit or delete my advert?

Yes, you can edit or delete your advert from your "My Adverts" dashboard at any time. Simply log in to your account and navigate to your active listings.

How do I search for a specific item?

You can use the search bar at the top of the page. You can also filter your search results by category, location, price, and condition to find exactly what you are looking for.

How do I contact a seller or buyer?

You can contact a seller directly through the messaging system on the advert page. This keeps all communication secure.

Is there a mobile app for SCHOMART?

Yes! While we don't have a traditional app on the App Store or Google Play, you can install our **Progressive Web App (PWA)** directly on your mobile device. The PWA gives you an app-like experience with an icon on your home screen, offline access to certain features, and faster loading times, all without needing to download it from a store.

Account and Profile

How do I reset my password?

On the login page, click "Forgot Password." Enter your registered email address, and we will send you a link to reset your password.

Can I change my username?

No, once a username is set during registration, it cannot be changed. However, you can update other profile details like your name, phone number, and profile picture in your account settings.

How can I update my profile information?

Log in to your account and navigate to the "Profile Settings" section. You can update your contact information, location, and other personal details there.

Why should I verify my phone number?

Verifying your phone number adds a layer of trust and security to your profile. It shows other users you are a legitimate person, which can increase your chances of making a successful sale or purchase.

What is "ads limit"?

"Ads limit" refers to the maximum number of active advertisements you can have at any given time. This limit is determined by your account type (e.g., Free, Premium, Business). You can view your current limit on your dashboard.

How can I increase my ads limit?

To increase your ads limit, you can upgrade your account to a Premium or Business plan. These plans offer higher limits and additional features to help you sell more effectively.

What is a user rating?

User ratings and reviews are a way for the community to provide feedback on transactions. A high rating indicates that a user is reliable and trustworthy. Always check a user's ratings before making a deal.

How do I report a fake profile or scammer?

On the user's profile page or within a chat, there is a "Report" button. Click it and provide details about the suspicious activity. Our team will investigate and take appropriate action.

Can I save my favourite ads?

Yes, you can save ads to your personal "Saved Adverts" list. Simply click the bookmark icon on any advert to add it to your list for later viewing.

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account, please contact our support team from your registered email address. For security purposes, we require a verification process before permanently deleting an account.

Buying and Selling

How to buy an item on SCHOMART?

Buying is simple: find the item you want, click on the ad, and use the built-in messaging system to contact the seller. Discuss the price, meeting location, and payment method. Always follow our safety tips to ensure a secure transaction.

How to sell an item on SCHOMART?

To sell, create an advert with a detailed description and clear photos. Respond promptly to messages from interested buyers, negotiate a fair price, and arrange a safe meeting or delivery for the exchange.

Do you handle payments or transactions?

No, SCHOMART does not directly handle payments. We provide the platform for buyers and sellers to connect. You are responsible for arranging the payment method directly with the other party. We recommend using a secure and traceable payment method.

What are the accepted payment methods?

Common payment methods include cash on delivery, bank transfers, or mobile money. Always agree on a payment method with the seller or buyer before the transaction. We advise against paying in advance for items you haven't seen.

Can I sell services on SCHOMART?

Yes, you can sell a wide range of services, including tutoring, graphic design, photography, event planning, and more. List your service in the appropriate category with a clear description of what you offer.

What if the item is not as described?

If an item is not as described, you should first try to resolve the issue with the seller. If you are unable to reach an agreement, you can report the user and the advert to our support team for investigation.

Do you offer a delivery service?

SCHOMART does not have its own delivery service. However, many sellers on our platform offer delivery, or you can use a third-party delivery service. Please discuss and agree on delivery options directly with the seller.

What is a "Boosted Ad"?

A "Boosted Ad" is a premium service that gives your advert higher visibility. It places your ad in a prominent position, such as at the top of search results, to attract more views and potential buyers.

How can I get more views on my advert?

To get more views, use clear and high-quality photos, write a detailed and honest description, and consider using one of our premium services like Ad Boosting or a Pro Sales campaign.

What is the recommended pricing for my item?

To determine a fair price, we recommend searching for similar items on SCHOMART and other platforms. Consider the item's condition, age, and market demand when setting your price.

Safety and Security

How can I stay safe when meeting a seller/buyer?

Always meet in a public place, like a campus square or cafe. Tell a friend or family member where you are going and when you expect to return. Never go to a private residence or isolated area alone.

What should I do if I suspect fraud or a scam?

If you suspect a scam, stop all communication immediately and report the user or ad to us using the "Report" function. Do not share any personal or financial information.

Is my personal information safe?

We take your privacy seriously. Your personal information is protected by our privacy policy. We do not share your details with third parties without your consent. We encourage you to only share necessary information to complete a transaction.

Why is it important to keep communication on the platform?

Keeping communication on the SCHOMART platform ensures we have a record of your conversations. This record is vital for our support team to investigate any disputes, fraudulent activity, or safety concerns that may arise.

How can I report inappropriate content or illegal activity?

You can report any inappropriate content, illegal items, or suspicious activity by clicking the "Report" button on the advert or contacting our support team directly. Please provide as many details as possible to help our investigation.

Do you use third-party payment services?

While we don't handle payments ourselves, we may partner with secure third-party payment processors for our premium services. When making a payment on SCHOMART, always ensure you are on a secure and trusted page.

What is your policy on prohibited items?

We have a strict policy on prohibited items. This includes illegal drugs, weapons, counterfeit goods, and stolen property. You can find a complete list of prohibited items in our Terms of Service.

How can I verify a seller's credibility?

You can check a seller's credibility by looking at their profile ratings and reviews from previous transactions. A long-standing account with positive feedback is a good sign of a trustworthy seller.

What if I receive a suspicious link?

Never click on suspicious links sent to you by other users, especially if they are asking for personal information or payment details. Only use the official SCHOMART website and its built-in features for all communications and transactions.

What is the "dispute resolution" process?

If a dispute arises, you should first try to resolve it with the other party. If that fails, you can contact our support team with all the details and chat history. We will review the case and provide guidance or take action as per our policies.

Sponsored Services and Ad Plans

What are Sponsored Services?

Sponsored Services are paid features designed to enhance your advertising. They include options like "Boosted Ads," "Top Ad," and "Highlighted Ad" to give your listing more visibility and a better chance of selling quickly.

What is the difference between a free and a paid plan?

A free plan allows you to post a limited number of basic ads. Paid plans, like our Sponsored or Business options, offer higher ad limits, increased visibility, and access to more features for professional sellers.

How do I pay for sponsored services?

You can pay for sponsored services using various secure payment methods, including bank transfers, and other online payment methods. Follow the on-screen instructions during the checkout process.

Are sponsored services refundable?

In most cases, sponsored service fees are non-refundable. Please review our Terms of Service for our full refund policy before making a purchase.

What is the duration of an advert?

A standard advert remains active for a specific period of days chosen during the advert posting process. You will be notified before it expires and given the option to renew it.

What happens when my ad expires?

When your ad expires, it will no longer be visible to the public. You can find it in your "Expired Adverts" section and choose to either renew it or delete it.

Can I upgrade my ad plan after it's posted?

No, SCHOMART does not offer the ability to upgrade a live advert. To change your ad's plan or features, you will need to delete the current advert and create a new one with your desired sponsored options.

Can I pause my advert?

No, you cannot pause an active or live advert on SCHOMART. If you need to take an advert down temporarily, you must delete it from your dashboard. You can create a new advert when you are ready to sell again.

How do I know if my ad is performing well?

Your dashboard provides statistics on your ads, including the number of views, clicks, and messages received. You can use this data to track your ad's performance and decide if you want to promote it further.

Can I get help from customer service to post a sponsored advert?

Yes, our customer service team can assist you with posting a sponsored advert. This is a sponsored service that costs more than standard ad boosting. Please contact our support team to learn more about our sponsored advert packages and how we can help your listing get maximum visibility.
