Frequently Asked Questions

Popular

How do I contact Support Team?

To contact our Support Team, you can use our live chat or email us at support@jiji.ng. Live chat is the fastest way to get a response, and email is ideal for non-urgent cases. For detailed assistance, provide as much information as possible.

What does "ads limit" mean?

"Ads limit" refers to the maximum number of advertisements you can post at once. It is based on your subscription plan or account status. If you reach this limit, you will need to upgrade your account or wait until some ads expire to post more.

How can I sell on Jiji?

To sell on Jiji, you need to create an account, post your products with detailed descriptions, and manage your listings. To increase visibility, consider using premium services and promotions like Boost Packages or Pro Sales campaigns.

How to buy something on Jiji?

Buying on Jiji is easy. Browse through listings, contact the seller directly through the messaging feature, and arrange payment and delivery. Always ensure the legitimacy of the seller and product before making a purchase.

How to report illegal activity on Jiji?

If you notice illegal activity on Jiji, please report it immediately by using the "Report" feature on the listing or by contacting our support team with detailed information. This helps us maintain a safe marketplace for all users.

What are Premium Services?

Premium Services include features like boosted ad visibility, priority customer support, and enhanced ad placement. These services help you get more exposure and improve your chances of making a sale faster.

General Information

What is a "marketplace"?

A marketplace is an online platform where buyers and sellers can meet, interact, and trade goods or services. Jiji serves as a marketplace, allowing individuals and businesses to list products for sale.

Do you have any stores?

We do not have physical stores. Jiji is an online marketplace where sellers can list their products directly. Sellers range from individuals to businesses, providing a wide range of goods and services.

Do you have a delivery?

Jiji does not handle deliveries directly, but we provide the platform for sellers and buyers to communicate and arrange deliveries. Many sellers offer delivery services, or you can arrange delivery yourself with the seller.

Can I unsubscribe from the newsletter?

Yes, you can unsubscribe from our newsletter anytime. Just click on the unsubscribe link provided at the bottom of the email, or you can manage your email preferences through your account settings.
